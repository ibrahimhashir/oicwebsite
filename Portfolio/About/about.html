my name is Ibrahim Hashir

Hi, my name is Ibrahim Hahsir, I am PHP developer. I have been at OiC for some months now, and have thoroughly enjoyed myself here. I am an interesting, fun loving guy with a good sense of humor. I like the land and the nature, I like people. And, I like to laugh always.
